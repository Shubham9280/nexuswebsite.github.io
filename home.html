Why Data Science?

Data science is the process of extracting knowledge from data. It is a rapidly growing field that is being used in a wide variety of industries, including healthcare, finance, and marketing. Data scientists use a variety of tools and techniques to analyze data, including machine learning, statistical analysis, and natural language processing.
There are many reasons why data science is important. First, data is becoming increasingly abundant. In the past, data was often difficult to collect and store. However, with the advent of new technologies, data can now be collected from a variety of sources, including sensors, social media, and financial transactions. This abundance of data presents an opportunity for data scientists to extract valuable insights that can be used to improve decision-making.
